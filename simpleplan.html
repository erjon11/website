Simple Plan

Tired of trying off the shelf diet plans, and every new type of exercise class to lose weight or tone up? Then the Simple Plan is for you. There are no magic formulas here. Instead, we hook you up with our expert advisor teams. Each team consists of a dietitian and a physical trainer who work together to assess you on your individual ability find out from you where you’d like to get to in terms of your own fitness goals. On your first visit we’ll also be sure you see a sports doctor for a full checkup just to make sure everything’s good ‘under the hood’.

Once we know what you’re made of, and where you’d like to get to, we can put it all together with our expertise and provide you with a weekly diet and exercise regime. But it doesn’t end there. We’ll make sure to keep up with your progress by making sure you get to see your team of experts regularly. That way, as you progress and your needs change we can make sure you’ve got the most up to date information to maximize the effect of the time you spend exercising.

Our diet recommendations don’t require you to buy any specially branded, over-priced products. We even provide you with example shopping lists using readily available supermarket products recommended by your personal dietitian.

Pricing for the Simple Plan is very competitive. For a limited time only your initial consultation as well as 30 days of gym access will be TOTALLY FREE.

Following your 30-day free trial, if you’d like to continue, you can retain gym access and two meetings a month with your advisor team for a very reasonable £50/month.
