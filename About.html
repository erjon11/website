About Us

About us

What we do

We’re here to take your headaches away when it comes to getting healthy and keeping fit. Our teams of experts are here to create a tailored diet and workout solution for you, incorporating your health and fitness desires into our ‘Simple Plan’.

As a member of the Simple Plan, you will not only have a personal trainer, but also a dietitian assigned to you. Their job is to work together to help you achieve your health and fitness desires, whether you’re looking to build your physical strength, work on your aesthetic tone, or increase your stamina and energy levels.

Our personal trainers are highly experienced experts when it comes to exercise prescription and instruction. That means they’re not just going to tell you what to do, but they’ll show you how and monitor your ability, giving you the peace of mind of knowing you’re getting the maximum effect from the time you put in to working out.

With our dietitian on hand to keep an eye on your progress, and make adjustments as necessary, you know that your intake will be perfectly aligned with your work out regime, giving you the exact balance of protein and energy you need, both for your work out, and your daily life.

Why we do it

“Health is wealth” is what we truly believe here at Simple Gym. We get satisfaction from knowing that we’ve made a positive difference to your lives, through our expertise and your hard work. Unfortunately, what we’ve noticed is that our competitors are often more interested in selling the product with the highest paying commissions rather than giving you well grounded unbiased advice.

How many times have you been promised that if you purchase a particular diet, or attend a particular fitness class you’ll gain everything from amazing strength and stamina to a toned body? Isn’t it suspicious that they seem to think the same solution will work for everyone?

Frustrated by the constant misinformation that gets thrown around today by false advertisers we decided to set up a place were people who understand that there is no magic solution could get real, personal advice as well as the facilities to work out. We know that getting and staying healthy takes real knowledge and hard work. We’re here to provide the knowledge, and the means, but you have to put in the hard work!

How we are different

Here at Simple Gym we know too well that we’re not all built the same. We wanted to create an environment that lets people of all abilities work together but also gives you a helping hand in decided what’s best for you. We hope that with this, you’ll be able to make the most of the time you spend in the gym, and not have to worry about the endless conflicting information from diet regime and fitness class vendors out there

This isn’t a magic formula, it just good old-fashioned hard work, combined with a personal diet plan, based on your current condition and your goals.

Our People

We are an equal opportunities employer and we pride ourselves on the fact that we draw our staff both from the local community as well as from a wider field of international experts.

All of our personal trainers are required to hold a level 4 or 5 REPs accredited health and fitness qualification. Our senior instructors have as a minimum of 5 years of professional experience, at least one of those being as a junior trainer here at Simple Gym.

Similarly our dietitians must be registered with the Health Professions Council in order to practice here. Senior dietitians hold post graduate qualifications accredited by the HPC, as well as having a minimum of 5 years experience as a junior or assistant dietitian.

Our goals

Our goal is to build a fitness community based on trust through hard work and knowledge.

We hope that everyone that visits us learns that getting healthy and keeping fit is really so simple that anyone can do it, and you don’t need any fancy equipment or magic formulas to make it work. If that means that you keep coming back then, in time, we hope you look to our trainers and advisors as mentors.

We hope that as you progress and achieve the goals you set, you will go on to become role models to your peers, inspiring them to come and see what they could achieve with the Simple Plan.

Contact Us

Whether you’re sure you’d like to join us, or if you’d just like some more information and maybe a chat, feel free to drop in or give us a call. Or, if you’d prefer, send us an email with any questions and we’ll get right back to you.
